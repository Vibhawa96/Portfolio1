About Me

I am an accomplished IT Project Manager, Business Analyst, Scrum Master and Data Analyst with proven track records of delivering innovative solutions across diverse industries. Throughout my career, I have worked with several organizations, delivering impactful projects that drive measurable results. At AIA Insurance, I led a group-level Data Governance project, achieving 65% data availability and 75% data quality. While at Unilever, I spearheaded the automation of 70% of HR processes, reducing paperwork by 50% and cutting IT infrastructure costs by 29%. Additionally, I successfully managed a core application upgrade at Allianz Insurance, which improved user satisfaction to 95% and reduced user incidents by 76%.

My expertise spans Agile project management, stakeholder management, risk mitigation, and delivering end-to-end solutions. I have hands-on experience conducting Scrum ceremonies, performing data analysis and visualizations, and developing Power Platform applications to streamline processes and improve decision-making. Leveraging tools like MS Power Platform, JIRA, and Confluence, I excel at transforming complex requirements into actionable strategies, ensuring seamless execution and enhanced team productivity.

In addition to my professional experience, I hold a Master of Business Administration in Project Management from Cardiff Metropolitan University, UK, and a Bachelor of Industrial Information Technology from Uva Wellassa University of Sri Lanka.

Driven by a passion for innovation and collaboration, I bring analytical thinking, strong decision-making, and leadership skills to every project. My academic and professional background equips me to bridge technical solutions with strategic business goals, creating meaningful impact for stakeholders and organisations alike. .
